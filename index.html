Your Journey with Sharing Yatra

A simple, step-by-step guide to booking your ride and saving money.

1. Search for Your Ride

Log in, enter your route, and choose your booking mode: 'Express Connect' for now or 'Schedule Booking' for later.

2. See Your Options

The system shows you two choices: available agencies to book a new 'Main Ride' OR existing 'Shared Rides' you can join at a discount.

Option 1: Book 'Main Ride'

You select an agency. Your ride status becomes 'Pending' while the agency manually approves it.

Option 2: Join 'Shared Ride'

You select an existing ride. The system checks for seats and... it's Auto-Approved! Your status is instantly 'Approved'.

3. Ride Confirmed!

Once 'Approved' (either way), your ride is confirmed. All you have to do is show up at the scheduled time.

4. Complete & Favourite

After your ride is 'Completed', you can view it in your history and add the agency or driver to your 'Favourites' to re-book with them later.
